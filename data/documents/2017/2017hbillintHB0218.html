7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill allows an election officer to establish an early voting polling place or an
10     election day voting center after certain statutory deadlines.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     allows an election officer to establish an early voting center or an election day
14     voting center after certain statutory deadlines have passed if certain conditions are
15     met;
16          ▸     amends requirements for an election notice, and the voter information pamphlet,
17     regarding certain polling location information; and
18          ▸     makes technical changes.
19     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
20          None
21     Other Special Clauses:
22          None

52          (c) that includes the information required by Subsections (3) and (4).
53          [(3) The notice and voter information pamphlet required by this section]

82          (6) (a) The governing body may mail a notice printed on a postage prepaid,
83     preaddressed return form that a person may use to request delivery of a voter information

106          (i) can be scheduled for use during early voting hours;
107          (ii) has the physical facilities necessary to accommodate early voting requirements;

113     election officer may designate additional early voting polling places [during the early voting

198          (c) the polling places for each voting precinct;
