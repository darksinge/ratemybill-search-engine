7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Utah Controlled Substances Act regarding Schedule I.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     adds the substance
13     3,4-dichloro-N-[2-(dimethylamino)cyclohexyl]-N-methylbenzamide that is also
14     known as U-47700 or "pink" to the list of controlled substances under Schedule I.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          This bill provides a special effective date.
