7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill designates a portion of Highway 85 in Salt Lake County as Minuteman
10     Highway.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     designates the portion of Highway 85 between Porter Rockwell Boulevard in
14     Herriman and 7800 South in West Jordan as Minuteman Highway.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          None
19     Utah Code Sections Affected:
20     ENACTS:
21          72-4-217, Utah Code Annotated 1953
22     

23     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
24          Section 1. Section 72-4-217 is enacted to read:
25          72-4-217. Minuteman Highway.
26          (1) There is established the Minuteman Highway composed of the existing Highway 85
27     in Salt Lake County from Porter Rockwell Boulevard to 7800 South in West Jordan.
28          (2) In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall
29     designate the portions of the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the Minuteman Highway
30     on future state highway maps.
