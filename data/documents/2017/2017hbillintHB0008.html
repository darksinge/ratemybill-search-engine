8     LONG TITLE
9     Committee Note:
10          The Executive Appropriations Committee recommended this bill.
11     General Description:
12          This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and
13     operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2017 and ending June 30, 2018.
14     Highlighted Provisions:
15          This bill:
16          ▸     provides funding for a 1.0% general salary increase for state and higher education
17     employees;
18          ▸     provides funding for a 8.0% increase in health insurance benefits rates for state and
19     higher education employees;
20          ▸     provides funding for retirement rate changes for certain state employees;
21          ▸     provides funding for an up-to $26 per pay period match for qualifying state employees
22     enrolled in a defined contribution plan; and
23          ▸     provides funding for other compensation adjustments as authorized.
24     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
25          This bill appropriates $59,957,500 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2018,
26     including:
27          ▸     $14,722,400 from the General Fund;
28          ▸     $14,907,700 from the Education Fund;
29          ▸     $30,327,400 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
30          This bill appropriates $117,300 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2018.
31          This bill appropriates $180,200 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2018.
32     Other Special Clauses:
33          This bill takes effect on July 1, 2017.
