8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10          This joint resolution of the Legislature requests that Utah's congressional delegation
11     submit federal legislation amending federal block grant maintenance-of-effort
12     requirements.
13     Highlighted Provisions:
14          This resolution:
15          ▸     recognizes that federal maintenance-of-effort requirements can severely limit state
16     budget flexibility; and
17          ▸     requests that Utah's congressional delegation submit federal legislation adding the
18     following language to each maintenance-of-effort statutory requirement: "In nowise
19     shall the state amount obligated as a maintenance-of-effort calculation exceed the
20     amount of federal funding provided through the grant."
21     Special Clauses:
22          None
23     

24     Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
25          WHEREAS, block grants offered by the federal government often include
26     maintenance-of-effort provisions specifying required state spending levels necessary to receive
27     a grant;
28          WHEREAS, if a state does not meet its maintenance-of-effort requirements in any
29     fiscal year, the federal government will often reduce dollar-for-dollar the state's federal block
30     grant in the following year;
31          WHEREAS, maintenance-of-effort requirements can be several times larger than the
32     amount of the federal block grant;
33          WHEREAS, large maintenance-of-effort requirements can severely limit state
34     legislative budget flexibility, especially during downturns;
35          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $3.2 million Mental Health Block
36     Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of $29.3 million;
37          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $5 million Baby Watch Early
38     Intervention Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of $12 million;
39          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $16.5 million Substance Abuse
40     Prevention and Treatment Block Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of
41     $19.2 million; and
42          WHEREAS, the state of Utah obligates hundreds of millions of dollars in
43     maintenance-of-effort requirements:
44          NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah
45     requests that Utah's congressional delegation submit federal legislation amending federal block
46     grant maintenance-of-effort requirements by adding the following language to each
47     maintenance-of-effort statutory requirement: "In nowise shall the state amount obligated as a
48     maintenance-of-effort calculation exceed the amount of federal funding provided through the
49     grant."
50          BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the President of
51     the United States, the Majority Leader of the United States Senate, the Speaker of the United
52     States House of Representatives, and the members of Utah's congressional delegation.
