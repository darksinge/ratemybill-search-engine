8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10           This bill establishes limits on federally authorized partial filling of Schedule II
11     controlled substance prescriptions.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸      specifies the conditions under which certain Schedule II controlled substance
15     prescriptions may be partially filled; and
16          ▸     requires rulemaking.
17     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
18          None
19     Other Special Clauses:
20          None

25     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
26          Section 1. Section 58-17b-610.6 is enacted to read:
27          58-17b-610.6. Partial filling of a Schedule II controlled substance prescription .
28          (1) For purposes of this section, "Schedule II controlled substance" means a substance
29     classified as a Schedule II controlled substance by the federal Controlled Substances Act, Title
30     II, Pub.L. No. 91-513 et seq., or Section 58-37-4.
31          (2) A prescription for a Schedule II controlled substance for a patient in a long-term
32     care facility or a patient with a terminal illness may be partially filled in accordance with
33     federal law.
34          (3) A prescription for a Schedule II controlled substance for a patient other than a
35     patient described in Subsection (2) may be partially filled:
36          (a) at the request of the patient or the practitioner who issued the prescription;
37          (b) not more than twice;
38          (c) unless the practitioner specifies otherwise:
39          (i) the first time in an amount equal to, or closely approximating, 25% of the supply
40     prescribed; and
41          (ii) the second time in an amount equal to, or closely approximating, 75% of the supply
42     prescribed;
43          (d) for the first time or a second time no later than 30 days after the prescription is
44     issued;
45          (e) only if the amount prescribed does not exceed a 14-day supply; and
46          (f) in accordance with federal law and rules made under Subsection (5).
47          (4) The sum of the amounts dispensed by the partial filling of a prescription under
48     Subsection (3) may not exceed the total amount prescribed.
49          (5) The division shall makes rules in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah
50     Administrative Rulemaking Act:
51          (a) specifying how to record the date, quantity supplied, and quantity remaining of a
52     prescription partially filled under Subsection (3); and
53          (b) otherwise necessary for the implementation of Subsections (2) and (3).
