8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10          This bill authorizes a Utah Intracurricular Student Organization Support for
11     Agricultural Education and Leadership support special group license plate.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸     creates a Utah Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural
15     Education and Leadership support special group license plate to help support
16     Utah-based chapters of certain student organizations that promote leadership and
17     career development through agricultural education;
18          ▸     requires applicants for the plate to make a $25 annual donation to the Utah
19     Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural Education and
20     Leadership Restricted Account;
21          ▸     creates the Utah Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural
22     Education and Leadership Restricted Account;
23          ▸     requires the Department of Agriculture and Food to distribute funds in the Utah
24     Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural Education and
25     Leadership Restricted Account to certain statewide student organizations that
26     promote leadership and career development through agricultural education; and
27          ▸     makes technical changes.
28     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
29          None

31          None

46          4-42-101. Title.
47          This chapter is known as the "Utah Intracurricular Student Organization Support for

49          Section 2. Section 4-42-102 is enacted to read:
50          4-42-102. Utah Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural
51     Education and Leadership Restricted Account.
52          (1) There is created in the General Fund a restricted account known as the "Utah
53     Intracurricular Student Organization Support for Agricultural Education and Leadership
54     Restricted Account."
55          (2) The account shall be funded by:
56          (a) contributions deposited into the account in accordance with Section 41-1a-422;
57          (b) private contributions; and
58          (c) donations or grants from public or private entities.
59          (3) Upon appropriation by the Legislature, the department shall distribute funds in the
60     account to one or more organizations that:
61          (a) are statewide agricultural education and leadership organizations; and
62          (b) promote leadership and career development through agricultural education.
63          (4) (a) An organization described in Subsection (3) may apply to the department to
64     receive a distribution in accordance with Subsection (3).
65          (b) An organization that receives a distribution from the department in accordance with
66     Subsection (3) shall expend the distribution only to:
67          (i) create or support programs that focus on issues described in Subsection (3);
68          (ii) create or sponsor programs that will benefit residents within the state; and
69          (iii) pay the costs of issuing or reordering Utah Intracurricular Student Organization
70     Support for Agricultural Education and Leadership special group license plate decals.
71          (c) In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the
72     department may make rules providing procedures for an organization to apply to the
73     department to receive a distribution under this Subsection (4).
74          (5) In accordance with Section 63J-1-602.1, appropriations from the account are
75     nonlapsing.
