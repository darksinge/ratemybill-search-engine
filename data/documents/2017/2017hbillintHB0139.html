7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill eliminates the defense of voluntary intoxication in a criminal action.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     eliminates the defense of voluntary intoxication in a criminal prosecution.
13     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
14          None
15     Other Special Clauses:
16          None
17     Utah Code Sections Affected:
18     AMENDS:
19          76-2-306, as enacted by Laws of Utah 1973, Chapter 196
20     

21     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
22          Section 1. Section 76-2-306 is amended to read:
23          76-2-306. Voluntary intoxication.
24          Voluntary intoxication [shall] is not [be] a defense to a criminal charge [unless such
25     intoxication negates the existence of the mental state which is an element of the offense;
26     however, if recklessness or criminal negligence establishes an element of an offense and the
27     actor is unaware of the risk because of voluntary intoxication, his unawareness is immaterial in
28     a prosecution for that offense.].
