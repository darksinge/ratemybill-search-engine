8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10          This bill extends the sunset date on the Crime Victim Reparations and Assistance
11     Board.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸     extends the sunset date on the Crime Victim Reparations and Assistance Board to
15     July 1, 2027.
16     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
17          None
18     Other Special Clauses:
19          None
