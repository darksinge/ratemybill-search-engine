             8      LONG TITLE
             9      General Description:
             10          This bill modifies the Insurance Code to enact the Portable Electronics Insurance Act,
             11      address adjusting of portable electronics insurance claims, and make conforming
             12      amendments.
             13      Highlighted Provisions:
             14          This bill:
             15          .    modifies definitions;
             16          .    enacts the Portable Electronics Insurance Act, including:
             17              .    defining terms;
             18              .    requiring vendors to hold a limited lines license to sell or offer coverage under a
             19      portable electronics insurance policy;
             20              .    providing for application and fees to obtain limited lines license;
             21              .    addressing employees and authorized representatives of vendors;
             22              .    providing for penalties for violation of part;
             23              .    imposing requirements for the sale of portable electronics insurance;
             24              .    addressing terminating or changing a portable electronics insurance policy; and
             25              .    addressing billing;
             26          .    addresses portable electronics claims adjusting and supervision of nonlicensed
             27      individuals; and

             28          .    makes technical and conforming changes.
             29      Money Appropriated in this Bill:
             30          None
             31      Other Special Clauses:
             32          This bill takes effect on July 1, 2012.
