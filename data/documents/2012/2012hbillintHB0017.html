             8      Committee Note:
             9          The Judiciary, Law Enforcement, and Criminal Justice Interim Committee
             10      recommended this bill.
             11      General Description:
             12          This bill creates the Sex and Kidnap Offender Registry chapter in Title 77.
             13      Highlighted Provisions:

             15          .    repeals Section 77-27-21.5 ; and
             16          .    creates a new chapter in Title 77, Code of Criminal Procedure, from its provisions.

             67          (b) release to the division all information obtained under Subsection (3)(a) relating to
             68      the applicant.

             70      fingerprints submitted under Subsection (2) and notify the division when a new entry is made
             71      in the applicable state and regional database against a person whose fingerprints are held in the
             72      file regarding any matter involving an arrest under state law involving:

             75          (iii) fraud; or

             78      division whether a person whose arrest was reported to the division under Subsection (4)(a)
             79      was subsequently convicted of the charge for which the person was arrested.
             80          (5) In addition to any fees imposed under this chapter, the division shall:
             81          (a) impose on individuals submitting fingerprints in accordance with this section the
             82      fees that the Bureau of Criminal Identification is authorized to collect for the services the
             83      Bureau of Criminal Identification provides under Subsections (3) and (4); and
             84          (b) remit the fees collected under Subsection (5)(a) to the Bureau of Criminal
             85      Identification.

             87      Identification under this section to determine whether a license should be denied, suspended, or
             88      revoked under Section 41-3-209 .

             120      effect until a final resolution is reached by the court involved or the charges are dropped.

             122      this section, the administrator shall provide the applicant written notice of the reason for the

             309          (13) A person who has an appointment with the division for testing and fails to keep
