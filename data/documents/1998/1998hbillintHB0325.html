ASSUMED NAMES FOR BUSINESSES

2    
1998 GENERAL SESSION

3    
STATE OF UTAH

4    
Sponsor: Beverly Ann Evans

5    AN ACT RELATING TO NAMES, CONDUCTING BUSINESS UNDER ASSUMED NAME;
6    EXPANDING THE FILING REQUIREMENTS; AMENDING PROHIBITIONS AGAINST
7    CERTAIN USES OF "CORPORATION" AND OTHER DESIGNATIONS; REQUIRING
8    NOTICE TO LOCAL LICENSING AUTHORITY; PROVIDING PENALTIES; AND
9    MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES.

6    to July 1, 1963, shall set forth:
7        (a) the name of the corporation;
8        (b) the period of duration, which may be perpetual;
9        (c) the purposes of organizing the corporation, including the fact that it is organized as a

11        (d) whether the corporation has members;
12        (e) if the corporation is to issue shares of stock evidencing membership in the corporation

15    shares of each class, the designation of each class and a statement of the preferences, limitations,
