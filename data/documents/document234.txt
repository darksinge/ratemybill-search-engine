     General Description             This bill modifies provisions regarding the release of recordings made by body cameras       worn by law enforcement officers        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides that any release of recordings made by a body camera that is worn by a law       enforcement officer shall be subject to the Government Records Access and       Management Act                   allows a requestor to immediately appeal to a district court any denial of access to a       recording if that denial is based solely on the grounds of a pending criminal action        and                  provides that a respondent government entity has five business days to request that       an appeal be assigned to the same judge who has jurisdiction over the pending       criminal case related to the requested recordings        Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             None       Utah Code Sections
