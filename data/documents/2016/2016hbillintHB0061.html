7     LONG TITLE
8     Committee Note:
9          The Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee recommended this bill.
10     General Description:
11          This bill addresses corporate franchise and income taxes.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸     addresses the apportionment of business income to the state for purposes of
15     corporate franchise and income taxes; and
16          ▸     makes technical and conforming changes.
17     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
18          None
19     Other Special Clauses:
20          This bill provides retrospective operation.

27     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
28          Section 1. Section 59-7-110 is amended to read:
29          59-7-110. Utah net losses -- Carryforwards and carrybacks -- Deduction.
30          (1) The amount of Utah net loss that shall be carried back or forward to offset income
31     of another taxable year is determined as provided in this section.
32          (2) (a) Subject to the other provisions of this section, a Utah net loss from a taxable
33     year beginning before January 1, 1994, shall be carried back three taxable years preceding the
34     taxable year of the loss and any remaining loss shall be carried forward five taxable years
35     following the taxable year of the loss.
36          (b) (i) Subject to the other provisions of this section, a Utah net loss from a taxable
37     year beginning on or after January 1, 1994, may be carried back three taxable years preceding
38     the taxable year of the loss and carried forward 15 taxable years following the taxable year of
39     the loss.
40          (ii) If an election is made to forego the federal net operating loss carryback, a Utah net
41     loss is not eligible to be carried back unless an election is made for state purposes.
42          (3) A Utah net loss shall be carried to the earliest eligible year for which the Utah
43     taxable income before net loss deduction, minus Utah net losses from previous years that were
44     applied or required to be applied to offset income, is not less than zero.
45          (4) (a) Except as provided in Subsection (4)(b), the amount of Utah net loss that shall
46     be carried to the year identified in Subsection (3) is the lesser of:
47          (i) the remaining Utah net loss after deduction of any amounts of the Utah net loss that
48     were carried to previous years; or
49          (ii) the remaining Utah taxable income before net loss deduction of the year identified
50     in Subsection (3) after deduction of Utah net losses from previous years that were carried or
51     required to be carried to the year identified in Subsection (3).
52          (b) (i) The amount of Utah net loss carried back from a taxable year may not exceed
53     $1,000,000 in Utah taxable income for each return filed under this chapter in a taxable year.
54          (ii) A Utah net loss in excess of $1,000,000 may be carried forward.
55          (iii) A remaining Utah net loss shall be available to be carried to one or more taxable
56     years in accordance with this section.
57          (5) (a) (i) Subject to Subsection (5)(a)(ii), a corporation acquiring the assets or stock of
58     another corporation may not deduct any net loss incurred by the acquired corporation prior to
59     the date of acquisition.
60          (ii) Subsection (5)(a)(i) does not apply if the only change in the corporation is that of
61     the state of incorporation.
62          (b) An acquired corporation may deduct the acquired corporation's net losses incurred
63     before the date of acquisition against the acquired corporation's separate income as calculated
64     under Subsections (6) and (7) if the acquired corporation has continued to carry on a trade or
65     business substantially the same as that conducted before the acquisition.
66          (6) For purposes of Subsection (5)(b), the amount of net loss an acquired corporation
67     that is acquired by a unitary group may deduct is calculated by:
68          (a) subject to Subsection (7):
69          (i) except as provided in Subsection (6)(a)(ii), calculating the sum of:
70          (A) an amount determined by dividing the average value of the acquired corporation's
71     real and tangible personal property owned or rented and used in this state during the taxable
72     year by the average value of all of the unitary group's real and tangible personal property owned
73     or rented and used during the taxable year;
74          (B) an amount determined by dividing the total amount paid in this state during the
75     taxable year by the acquired corporation for compensation by the total compensation paid
76     everywhere by the unitary group during the taxable year; and
77          (C) an amount determined by:
78          (I) dividing the total sales of the acquired corporation in this state during the taxable
79     year by the total sales of the unitary group everywhere during the taxable year; and
80          (II) [(Aa)] if the unitary group elects to calculate the fraction for apportioning business
81     income to this state using the method described in Subsection 59-7-311(2)[(d)](b), multiplying
82     the amount calculated under Subsection (6)(a)(i)(C)(I) by two; or
83          [(Bb) if the unitary group is required to calculate the fraction for apportioning business
84     income to this state using the method described in Subsection 59-7-311(3)(a), multiplying the
85     amount calculated under Subsection (6)(a)(i)(C)(I) by four; or]
86          [(Cc) if the unitary group is required to calculate the fraction for apportioning business
87     income to this state using the method described in Subsection 59-7-311(3)(b), multiplying the
88     amount calculated under Subsection (6)(a)(i)(C)(I) by 10; or]
89          (ii) if the unitary group [is required] elects to calculate the fraction for apportioning
90     business income to this state using the method described in Subsection 59-7-311[(3)(c)](2)(c),
91     calculating an amount determined by dividing the total sales of the acquired corporation in this
92     state during the taxable year by the total sales of the unitary group everywhere during the
93     taxable year;
94          (b) dividing the amount calculated under Subsection (6)(a) by the same denominator of
95     the fraction the unitary group uses to apportion business income to this state:
96          (i) for that taxable year; and
97          (ii) in accordance with Section 59-7-311;
98          (c) multiplying the amount calculated under Subsection (6)(b) by the business income
99     of the unitary group for the taxable year that is subject to apportionment under Section
100     59-7-311; and
101          (d) calculating the sum of:
102          (i) the amount calculated under Subsection (6)(c); and
103          (ii) the following amounts allocable to the acquired corporation for the taxable year:
104          (A) nonbusiness income allocable to this state; or
105          (B) nonbusiness loss allocable to this state.
106          (7) The amounts calculated under Subsection (6)(a) shall be derived in the same
107     manner as those amounts are derived for purposes of apportioning the unitary group's business
108     income before deducting the net loss, including a modification made in accordance with
109     Section 59-7-320.
