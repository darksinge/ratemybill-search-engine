7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill amends provisions related to records that are subject to federal law.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     exempts certain education records from the Government Records and Access
13     Management Act; and
14          ▸     makes technical and conforming changes.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          None
19     Utah Code Sections Affected:
20     AMENDS:
21          63G-2-107, as renumbered and amended by Laws of Utah 2008, Chapter 382
22     

23     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
24          Section 1. Section 63G-2-107 is amended to read:
25          63G-2-107. Disclosure of records subject to federal law.
26          Notwithstanding [the provisions of Subsections 63G-2-201(6)(a) and (b)] Subsection
27     63G-2-201(6), this chapter does not apply to:
28          (1) a record containing protected health information as defined in 45 C.F.R., Part 164,
29     Standards for Privacy of Individually Identifiable Health Information, if the record is:
30          [(1)] (a) controlled or maintained by a governmental entity; and
31          [(2)] (b) governed by 45 C.F.R., Parts 160 and 164, Standards for Privacy of
32     Individually Identifiable Health Information[.]; or
33          (2) an education record as defined in Family Educational Rights and Privacy, 34 C.F.R.
34     Part 99, if the record is:
35          (a) controlled or maintained by a governmental entity; and
36          (b) governed by Family Educational Rights and Privacy, 34 C.F.R. Part 99.
