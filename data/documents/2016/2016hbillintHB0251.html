29     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
30          Section 1. Section 34-51-101 is enacted to read:
31     
CHAPTER 51. POST-EMPLOYMENT RESTRICTIONS ACT

32     
Part 1. General Provisions

33          34-51-101. Title.
34          This chapter is known as the "Post-Employment Restrictions Act."
35          Section 2. Section 34-51-102 is enacted to read:
36          34-51-102. Definitions.
37          As used in this chapter, "post-employment restriction" means a condition an employer
38     imposes on an employee, whether by agreement, policy, or otherwise, that restricts the
39     employee's actions after termination of employment.
40          Section 3. Section 34-51-201 is enacted to read:
41     
Part 2. Post-Employment Restrictions

42          34-51-201. Prohibited post-employment restrictions.
43          (1) A post-employment restriction may not include any of the following:
44          (a) a restriction that, after termination of employment, the employee may not provide
45     products, processes, or services that are similar to the employer's products, processes, or
46     services or otherwise work in the same industry as the employer's for any period that extends
47     beyond the termination of employment;
48          (b) a restriction that, after termination of employment, the employee may not, either
49     directly or indirectly, own an interest in an entity that provides products, processes, or services
50     that are similar to the employer's products, processes, or services; or
51          (c) a restriction that gives an employer the right to require an employee to solely and
52     exclusively negotiate an extension of the employment agreement with the employer.
53          (2) This section may not be interpreted as prohibiting an employer or employee from
54     entering into an employment contract that includes post-employment restrictions not prohibited
55     by this section.
56          Section 4. Section 34-51-202 is enacted to read:
57          34-51-202. Scope of chapter.
58          Consistent with this chapter, a court of competent jurisdiction or an arbitrator, if the
59     post-employment restriction provides for arbitration, may determine whether a
60     post-employment restriction is enforceable.
61          Section 5. Section 34-51-301 is enacted to read:
62     
Part 3. Remedies

63          34-51-301. Award of arbitration costs, attorney fees and court costs, and damages.
64          If an employer seeks to enforce a post-employment restriction through arbitration or by
65     filing a court action and it is determined that the post-employment restriction is prohibited
66     under Section 34-51-201, the employer is liable for the former employee's:
67          (1) (a) if enforcement is sought through arbitration, costs associated with arbitration; or
68          (b) if the employer files a court action, attorney fees and court costs; and
69          (2) actual damages.
