7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Property Tax Act to address providing notices.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     permits the county auditor to provide certain notices by electronic mail if certain
13     conditions are met; and
14          ▸     makes conforming and technical changes.
