7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill enacts provisions of the Utah Health Care Malpractice Act.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     provides requirements for a nonpatient plaintiff to establish a malpractice action
13     against a health care provider.
14     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
15          None
16     Other Special Clauses:
17          None
18     Utah Code Sections Affected:
19     ENACTS:
20          78B-3-426, Utah Code Annotated 1953
21     

22     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
23          Section 1. Section 78B-3-426 is enacted to read:
24          78B-3-426. Nonpatient cause of action.
25          To establish a malpractice action against a health care provider, a nonpatient plaintiff
26     shall be required to show that:
27          (1) the nonpatient plaintiff suffered an injury;
28          (2) the nonpatient plaintiff's injury was proximately caused by an act or omission of the
29     health care provider; and
30          (3) the health care provider's act or omission was conduct manifesting:
31          (a) knowing disregard for the safety of the nonpatient; or
32          (b) gross negligence that constitutes careless or reckless disregard to a degree that
33     shows indifference to the consequences that may result.
