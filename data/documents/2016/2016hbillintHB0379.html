8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10          This bill authorizes the State Board of Regents to execute an interstate reciprocity
11     agreement regarding postsecondary distance education.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸     authorizes the State Board of Regents to execute an interstate reciprocity agreement
15     regarding interstate offering of postsecondary distance education courses and
16     programs; and
17          ▸     grants rulemaking authority.
18     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
19          None
20     Other Special Clauses:
21          None
22     Utah Code Sections Affected:
23     ENACTS:
24          53B-16-109, Utah Code Annotated 1953
25     

26     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
27          Section 1. Section 53B-16-109 is enacted to read:
28          53B-16-109. Interstate reciprocity agreement regarding postsecondary distance
29     education courses.
30          (1) As used in this section:
31          (a) "Distance education" means instruction offered by a means where the student and
32     faculty member are in separate physical locations.
33          (b) "Institution" means a degree-granting postsecondary education entity.
34          (c) "Postsecondary education" means education or educational services offered
35     primarily to an individual who:
36          (i) has completed or terminated the individual's secondary or high school education; or
37          (ii) is beyond the age of compulsory school attendance.
38          (2) The board may execute an interstate reciprocity agreement for postsecondary
39     distance education:
40          (a) for an institution that offers a postsecondary distance education course or program;
41     and
42          (b) that requires an institution to meet certain standards to become authorized to
43     operate under the interstate reciprocity agreement.
44          (3) If the board executes an interstate reciprocity agreement under Subsection (2), the
45     board shall make rules, in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative
46     Rulemaking Act, to establish:
47          (a) standards for granting an institution authorization to operate under the interstate
48     reciprocity agreement;
49          (b) a filing, document, or membership fee required for an institution to obtain
50     authorization under the interstate reciprocity agreement; and
51          (c) a process for administering the interstate reciprocity agreement.
