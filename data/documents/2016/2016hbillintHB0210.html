7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Traffic Code by amending media production vehicle provisions.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     requires a media production to provide advance notice to law enforcement when
13     using a media production vehicle on a highway;
14          ▸     provides for notice requirements; and
15          ▸     makes technical changes.
16     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
17          None
18     Other Special Clauses:
19          None
20     Utah Code Sections Affected:
21     AMENDS:
22          41-6a-1718, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2015, Chapter 405
23     

24     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
25          Section 1. Section 41-6a-1718 is amended to read:
26          41-6a-1718. Media production vehicle -- Definition -- Exemption -- Identification.
27          (1) As used in this section:
28          (a) "Media production" means the making of a motion picture, television show, video,
29     commercial, Internet video, or other viewable programming provided to viewers via a movie
30     theater or transmitted through broadcast radio wave, cable, satellite, wireless, or Internet.
31          (b) "Media production vehicle" means a vehicle used exclusively for media production.
32          (2) [A] If a media production entity using a media production vehicle provides at least
33     24 hours' advance notice as described in Subsection (3) to the law enforcement agency having
34     jurisdiction of the highway being used by the media production vehicle, the media production
35     vehicle is exempt from the restrictions of Section 41-6a-1616 while the vehicle is:
36          (a) being used to simulate an authorized emergency vehicle in [a] the media
37     production; or
38          (b) being driven in transit between the media production location and the media
39     production vehicle storage location if during transit the vehicle displays a sign prominently on
40     each front-side door of the media production vehicle stating "Media Production Vehicle."
41          (3) The notice described in Subsection (2) shall include:
42          (a) the date;
43          (b) the time;
44          (c) the designated route of travel and location of use; and
45          (d) a description of the media production vehicle.
