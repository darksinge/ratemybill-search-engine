12     General Description:
13          This bill modifies Department of Environmental Quality consumer electronic device
14     recycling reporting requirements.
15     Highlighted Provisions:
16          This bill:
17          ▸     modifies Department of Environmental Quality consumer electronic device
18     recycling reporting requirements.

20          None
21     Other Special Clauses:
22          None
23     Utah Code Sections Affected:
24     AMENDS:
25          19-6-1203, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 213
26     

27     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
28          Section 1. Section 19-6-1203 is amended to read:
29          19-6-1203. Reporting requirements.
30          (1) On or after July 1, 2011, a manufacturer may not offer a consumer electronic device
31     for sale in the state unless the manufacturer, either individually, through a group manufacturer
32     organization, or through the manufacturer's industry trade group, prepares and submits, subject
33     to Subsection (2), a report on or before August 1 of each year to the department.
34          (2) The report required under Subsection (1):
35          (a) shall include a list of eligible programs, subject to Subsection (3); and
36          (b) may include:
37          (i) an existing collection, transportation, or recycling system for a consumer electronic
38     device; and
39          (ii) an eligible program offered by:
40          (A) a consumer electronic device recycler;
41          (B) a consumer electronic device repair shop;
42          (C) a recycler of other commodities;
43          (D) a reuse organization;
44          (E) a not-for-profit corporation;
45          (F) a retailer; or
46          (G) another similar operation, including a local government collection event.
47          (3) The list required in Subsection (2)(a) may be in the form of a geographic map
48     identifying the type and location of an eligible program.
49          (4) The department shall:
50          (a) compile the report required under Subsection (1); and
51          (b) beginning on October 31, 2012, submit annually on or before October 31 the
52     compiled report to the [Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee
53     and the] Public Utilities and Technology Interim Committee.
