7     LONG TITLE
8     Committee Note:
9          The Executive Appropriations Committee recommended this bill.
10     General Description:
11          This bill appropriates funds for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal
12     years beginning July 1, 2015 and ending June 30, 2016 and beginning July 1, 2016 and ending June
13     30, 2017.
14     Highlighted Provisions:
15          This bill:
16          ▸     provides appropriations for the use and support of certain state agencies; and
17          ▸     provides appropriations for other purposes as described.
18     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
19          This bill appropriates ($118,200) in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2016, all of
20     which is from the General Fund.
21          This bill appropriates $118,200 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2016, all of
22     which is from the General Fund.
23          This bill appropriates $893,889,500 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2017,
24     including:
25          ▸     $642,816,000 from the General Fund;
26          ▸     $49,000 from the Education Fund;
27          ▸     $251,024,500 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
28          This bill appropriates $15,925,800 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2017.
29          This bill appropriates $28,784,500 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2017.
30          This bill appropriates $381,000 in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2017,
31     including:
32          ▸     $231,000 from the General Fund;
33          ▸     $150,000 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
34     Other Special Clauses:
35          Section 1 of this bill takes effect immediately. Section 2 of this bill take effect on July 1,
36     2016.
