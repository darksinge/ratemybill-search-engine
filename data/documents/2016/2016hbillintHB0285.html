7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies a provision relating to the Board of Examiners.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     eliminates a requirement that notice of Board of Examiners meetings be published
13     in newspapers; and
14          ▸     modifies language relating to the meeting of the Legislature.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          None
19     Utah Code Sections Affected:
20     AMENDS:
21          63G-9-303, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2010, Chapter 90
22     

23     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
24          Section 1. Section 63G-9-303 is amended to read:
25          63G-9-303. Meeting to examine claims -- Notice of meeting.
26          (1) At least 60 days preceding the [meeting of each] annual general session of the
27     Legislature, the board shall hold a session for the purpose of examining the claims referred to
28     in Section 63G-9-302, and may adjourn from time to time until the work is completed.
29          (2) The board shall cause notice of such meeting or meetings to be published[: (a) in
30     some newspaper at the seat of government and such other newspapers as may be determined by
31     the board for such time as the board may prescribe; and (b)] on the Utah Public Notice Website
32     created in Section 63F-1-701.
