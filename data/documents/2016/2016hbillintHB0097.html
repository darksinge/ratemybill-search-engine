7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill authorizes a children with cancer support special group license plate.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     creates a children with cancer support special group license plate for certain
13     organizations that create or support programs that provide assistance to children
14     with cancer;
15          ▸     requires applicants for a new plate to make a $25 annual donation to the Children
16     with Cancer Support Restricted Account;
17          ▸     creates the Children with Cancer Support Restricted Account;
18          ▸     requires the Department of Health to distribute funds in the Children with Cancer
19     Support Restricted Account to certain organizations that create or support programs
20     that provide assistance or support to children with cancer; and
21          ▸     makes technical changes.
22     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
23          None
24     Other Special Clauses:
25          This bill provides a special effective date.

34     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
35          Section 1. Section 26-21a-304 is enacted to read:
36          26-21a-304. Children with Cancer Support Restricted Account.
37          (1) As used in this section, "account" means the Children with Cancer Support
38     Restricted Account created in this section.
39          (2) There is created in the General Fund a restricted account known as the "Children
40     with Cancer Support Restricted Account."
41          (3) The account shall be funded by:
42          (a) contributions deposited into the account in accordance with Section 41-1a-422;
43          (b) private contributions;
44          (c) donations or grants from public or private entities; and
45          (d) interest and earnings on account money.
46          (4) The department shall distribute funds in the account to one or more charitable
47     organizations that:
48          (a) qualify as tax exempt under Section 501(c)(3), Internal Revenue Code;
49          (b) are hospitals for children's tertiary care with board certified pediatric hematologist
50     oncologists treating children, both on an inpatient and outpatient basis, with blood disorders
51     and cancers from throughout the state;
52          (c) are members of a national organization devoted exclusively to childhood and
53     adolescent cancer research;
54          (d) have pediatric nurses trained in hematology oncology;
55          (e) participate in one or more pediatric cancer clinical trials; and
56          (f) have programs that provide assistance to children with cancer.
57          (5) (a) An organization described in Subsection (4) may apply to the department to
58     receive a distribution in accordance with Subsection (4).
59          (b) An organization that receives a distribution from the department in accordance with
60     Subsection (4) may expend the distribution only to create or support programs that provide
61     assistance to children with cancer.
62          (c) In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the
63     department may make rules providing procedures for an organization to apply to the
64     department to receive a distribution under Subsection (4).
