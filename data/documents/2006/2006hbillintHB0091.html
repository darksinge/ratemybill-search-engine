H.B. 91

             1     

REDISTRICTING AMENDMENTS

             2     
2006 GENERAL SESSION

             3     
STATE OF UTAH

             4     
Chief Sponsor: Rosalind J. McGee

             5     
Senate Sponsor: ____________

             6     
             7      LONG TITLE
             8      General Description:
             9          This bill requires any redistricting plan to be adopted by at least a two-thirds vote.
             10      Highlighted Provisions:
             11          This bill:
             12          .    prohibits the Legislature from adopting a redistricting plan unless the plan is
             13      approved by more than two-thirds of both houses of the Legislature.
             14      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             15          None
             16      Other Special Clauses:
             17          None
             18      Utah Code Sections Affected:
             19      ENACTS:
             20          36-1a-101, Utah Code Annotated 1953
             21     
             22      Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
             23          Section 1. Section 36-1a-101 is enacted to read:
             24          36-1a-101. Redistricting procedures.
             25          The Legislature may not adopt a Congressional, State Senate, State House of
             26      Representatives, or State School Board redistricting plan unless the plan is approved by at least
             27      a two-thirds vote of both houses of the Legislature.
