H.B. 332

             1     

VETERANS SPECIAL GROUP LICENSE

             2     
PLATE AMENDMENTS

             3     
2004 GENERAL SESSION

             4     
STATE OF UTAH

             5     
Sponsor: Steven R. Mascaro

             6     
             7      LONG TITLE
             8      General Description:
             9          This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending special group license plate
             10      provisions.
             11      Highlighted Provisions:
             12          This bill:
             13          .    provides that a contributor to the Office of Veteran's Affairs shall make an annual
             14      donation of $10 for a veterans' special group license plate after the initial $25
             15      donation required at the time of application; and
             16          .    makes technical changes.
             17      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             18          None
             19      Other Special Clauses:
             20          This bill takes effect on October 1, 2004.
             21      Utah Code Sections Affected:
             22      AMENDS:
             23          41-1a-422, as last amended by Chapters 74 and 141, Laws of Utah 2003
             24     
             25      Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
             26          Section 1. Section 41-1a-422 is amended to read:
             27           41-1a-422. Support special group license plates -- Contributor -- Voluntary

             28      contribution collection procedures.
             29          (1) As used in this section:
             30          (a) (i) ["Contributor"] Except as provided in Subsection (1)(a)(ii), "contributor" means
             31      a person who has donated or in whose name at least $25 has been donated to:
             32          [(i)] (A) a scholastic scholarship fund of a single named institution;
             33          [(ii)] (B) the Division of Veterans' Affairs in the Utah National Guard for veterans'
             34      programs;
             35          [(iii)] (C) the Division of Wildlife Resources for the Wildlife Resources Account
             36      created in Section 23-14-13 , for conservation of wildlife and the enhancement, preservation,
             37      protection, access, and management of wildlife habitat;
             38          [(iv)] (D) the Department of Agriculture and Food for the benefit of soil conservation
             39      districts;
             40          [(v)] (E) the Division of Parks and Recreation for the benefit of snowmobile programs;
             41          [(vi)] (F) the Guardian Ad Litem Services Account and the Children's Museum of
             42      Utah, with the donation evenly divided between the two;
             43          [(vii)] (G) the Boy Scouts of America for the benefit of a Utah Boy Scouts of America
             44      council as specified by the contributor;
             45          [(viii)] (H) No More Homeless Pets in Utah for distribution to organizations or
             46      individuals that provide spay and neuter programs that subsidize the sterilization of domestic
             47      animals;
             48          [(ix)] (I) the Utah Alliance of Boys and Girls Clubs, Inc. to provide and enhance youth
             49      development programs; or
             50          [(x)] (J) the Utah Association of Public School Foundations to support public
             51      education.
             52          (ii) For a veteran's special group license plate, "contributor" means a person who has
             53      donated or in whose name at least a $25 donation at the time of application and $10 annual
             54      donation thereafter has been made.
             55          (b) "Institution" means a state institution of higher education as defined under Section
             56      53B-3-102 or a private institution of higher education in the state accredited by a regional or
             57      national accrediting agency recognized by the United States Department of Education.
             58          (2) (a) An applicant for original or renewal collegiate special group license plates under

             59      Subsection (1)(a)(i) must be a contributor to the institution named in the application and
             60      present the original contribution verification form under Subsection (2)(b) or make a
             61      contribution to the division at the time of application under Subsection (3).
             62          (b) An institution with a support special group license plate shall issue to a contributor
             63      a verification form designed by the commission containing:
             64          (i) the name of the contributor;
             65          (ii) the institution to which a donation was made;
             66          (iii) the date of the donation; and
             67          (iv) an attestation that the donation was for a scholastic scholarship.
             68          (c) The state auditor may audit each institution to verify that the moneys collected by
             69      the institutions from contributors are used for scholastic scholarships.
             70          (d) After an applicant has been issued collegiate license plates or renewal decals, the
             71      commission shall charge the institution whose plate was issued, a fee determined in accordance
             72      with Section 63-38-3.2 for management and administrative expenses incurred in issuing and
             73      renewing the collegiate license plates.
             74          (e) If the contribution is made at the time of application, the contribution shall be
             75      collected, treated, and deposited as provided under Subsection (3).
             76          (3) (a) [Except as provided under Subsection (3)(d), an] An applicant for original or
             77      renewal support special group license plates under this section must be [an annual] a
             78      contributor to the [entity or named institution] sponsoring organization associated with the
             79      license plate.
             80          (b) This contribution shall be:
             81          (i) unless collected by the named institution under Subsection (2), collected by the
             82      division;
             83          (ii) [treated as] considered a voluntary contribution for the funding of the activities
             84      specified under this section and not [as] a motor vehicle registration fee; and
             85          (iii) deposited into the appropriate account less actual administrative costs associated
             86      with issuing the license plates.
             87          (c) [Except as provided under Subsection (3)(d), the] The donation described in
             88      Subsection (1)(a) must be made in the 12 months prior to registration or renewal of
             89      registration.

             90          (d) The donation described in Subsection (1)(a) shall be a one-time donation made to
             91      the division when issuing original:
             92          [(i) veterans' license plates;]
             93          [(ii)] (i) snowmobile license plates; or
             94          [(iii)] (ii) soil conservation license plates.
             95          (4) Veterans' license plates shall display one of the symbols representing the Army,
             96      Navy, Air Force, Marines, Coast Guard, or American Legion.
             97          Section 2. Effective date.
             98          This bill takes effect on October 1, 2004.
