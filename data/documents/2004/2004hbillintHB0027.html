H.B. 27

             1     

INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAX AMENDMENTS

             2     
2004 GENERAL SESSION

             3     
STATE OF UTAH

             4     
Sponsor: Wayne A. Harper

             5     
             6      LONG TITLE
             7      General Description:
             8          This bill modifies the Individual Income Tax Act.
             9      Highlighted Provisions:
             10          This bill:
             11          .    provides that the Internal Revenue Code in effect on January 1, 2004 shall be used
             12      for certain purposes within the Individual Income Tax Act including:
             13              .    determining the state taxable income of a resident individual, trust, estate, or
             14      partnership;
             15              .    determining the amount subtracted from federal taxable income for the Higher
             16      Education Savings Incentive Program;
             17              .    determining the state income tax percentage for purposes of the tax on a
             18      nonresident's state taxable income;
             19              .    determining whether certain federal tax credits affect state income taxes;
             20              .    determining whether a taxpayer is exempt from taxation;
             21              .    determining whether a person is eligible for the tax credit for at-home parents;
             22      and
             23              .    determining whether a person is eligible for a subtraction from federal taxable
             24      income for certain retirement income; and
             25          .    makes technical changes.
             26      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             27          None

             28      Other Special Clauses:
             29          This bills has retrospective operation for taxable years beginning on or after January 1,
             30      2004.
