H.B. 70

             1     

GERIATRIC CARE MANAGERS

             2     
2004 GENERAL SESSION

             3     
STATE OF UTAH

             4     
Sponsor: Rebecca D. Lockhart

             5     
             6      LONG TITLE
             7      General Description:
             8          This bill amends the Nurse Practice Act to certify licensed practical nurses who have
             9      additional training in long-term care nursing as geriatric care managers and to repeal
             10      provisions in the Nurse Practice Act related to health care assistants.
             11      Highlighted Provisions:
             12          This bill:
             13          .    defines a geriatric care manager and the practice of geriatric care management;
             14          .    creates a pilot program for certification of geriatric care managers;
             15          .    specifies qualifications for geriatric care managers;
             16          .    repeals provisions related to health care assistants;
             17          .    amends provisions related to unlawful conduct to incorporate geriatric care
             18      managers;
             19          .    establishes standards for geriatric care manager training; and
             20          .    sunsets the Geriatric Care Manager Pilot Program on July 1, 2007.
             21      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             22          None
             23      Other Special Clauses:
             24          None
