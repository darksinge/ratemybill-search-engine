             20      LONG TITLE
             21      General Description:
             22          This bill modifies the Sales and Use Tax Act to amend sales and use tax exemption
             23      provisions.
             24      Highlighted Provisions:
             25          This bill:
             26          .    extends the sunset date by ten years for the sales and use tax exemption for sales or
             27      leases of rolls, rollers, refractory brick, electric motors, or other replacement parts
             28      used in the furnaces, mills, or ovens of certain steel mills.
             29      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             30          None
             31      Other Special Clauses:

             32          This bill takes effect on July 1, 2004.
