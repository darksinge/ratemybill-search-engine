H.B. 37

SUNSET REAUTHORIZATIONS

             2     
2004 GENERAL SESSION

             3     
STATE OF UTAH

             4     
Sponsor: Greg J. Curtis

             5     
             6      LONG TITLE
             7      General Description:
             8          This bill reauthorizes certain state entities and programs that would otherwise sunset
             9      before the 2005 Annual General Session of the Utah Legislature.
             10      Highlighted Provisions:
             11          This bill:
             12          .    reauthorizes state entities and programs that are scheduled to sunset before the 2005
             13      Annual General Session.
             14      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             15          None
             16      Other Special Clauses:
             17          None
