H.B. 3

             1           Supplemental Appropriations Act II
             2          2004 GENERAL SESSION
             3          STATE OF UTAH
             4          Sponsor: Ron Bigelow
             5      .......................................................... ...........
             6      LONG TITLE
             7      General Description:
             8          This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and operation
             9      of state government for the fiscal years beginning July 1, 2004 and ending June 30, 2005 and
             10      beginning July 1, 2003 and ending June 30, 2004.
             11      Highlighted Provisions:
             12          This bill:
             13          .    provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain state agencies;
             14          .    provides funds for the bills with fiscal impact passed in the 2004 General Session;
             15          .    provides budget increases and decreases for other purposes as described;
             16          .    provides intent language;
             17          .    amends previously approved internal service fund employment levels and capital
             18      acquisition amounts.
             19      Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
             20          This bill appropriates for fiscal year 2005:
             21          .    $52,358,200 from the General Fund;
             22          .    $173,100 from the Uniform School Fund;
             23          .    $13,865,500 from income tax revenue;
             24          .    $195,478,100 from various sources as detailed herein.
             25          This bill appropriates for fiscal year 2004:
             26          .    $28,570,000 from the General Fund;
             27          .    $1,000,000 from the Uniform School Fund;
             28          .    ($23,200,000) from income tax revenue;
             29          .    $329,400 from various sources as detailed herein.
             30      Other Special Clauses:
             31          Section 1 of this bill takes effect on July 1, 2004. Section 2 of this bill takes effect
             32      immediately.

             34          None
