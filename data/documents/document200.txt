         AGRICULTURE REGULATION PREEMPTION AMENDMENTS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Stephen G  Handy      Senate Sponsor    Evan J  Vickers             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill provides that state regulation of commercial feed  fertilizer  pesticides  and       seeds preempts local regulation        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   states that the Department of Agriculture and Food  subject to relevant federal law        has exclusive jurisdiction to regulate commercial feed  fertilizer  pesticides  and       seeds in the state                   states that the regulation of commercial feed  fertilizer  pesticides  and seeds is of       statewide concern and state code occupies the whole field of potential regulation        and                  states that a political subdivision of the state is prohibited from regulating       commercial feed  fertilizer  pesticides  or seeds  and local ordinances that seek to       regulate commercial feed  fertilizer  pesticides  or seeds are void        Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             None
