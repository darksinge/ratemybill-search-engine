H.B. 34

             1     

SPECIAL GROUP LICENSE PLATE AMENDMENTS

             2     
2013 GENERAL SESSION

             3     
STATE OF UTAH

             4     
Chief Sponsor: Val L. Peterson

             5     
Senate Sponsor: Todd Weiler

             6     
             7      LONG TITLE
             8      Committee Note:
             9          The Transportation Interim Committee recommended this bill.
             10      General Description:
             11          This bill authorizes an In God We Trust freedom celebration support special group
             12      license plate.
             13      Highlighted Provisions:
             14          This bill:
             15          .    authorizes an In God We Trust freedom celebration support special group license
             16      plate for programs that create or support educational and community events within
             17      the state that celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country;
             18          .    requires applicants for a new plate to make a $25 annual donation to the In God We
             19      Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account;
             20          .    creates the In God We Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account;
             21          .    requires the Department of Heritage and Arts to distribute funds in the In God We
             22      Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account to certain organizations that
             23      provide programs that create or support educational and community events within
             24      the state that celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country; and
             25          .    makes technical changes.
             26      Money Appropriated in this Bill:
             27          None

             28      Other Special Clauses:
             29          This bill takes effect on October 1, 2013.
             30      Utah Code Sections Affected:
             31      AMENDS:

CHAPTER 19. IN GOD WE TRUST FREEDOM CELEBRATION SUPPORT

             42     
RESTRICTED ACCOUNT ACT

             43          9-19-101. Title.
             44          This chapter is known as the "In God We Trust Freedom Celebration Support
             45      Restricted Account Act."
             46          Section 2. Section 9-19-102 is enacted to read:
             47          9-19-102. In God We Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account.
             48          (1) There is created in the General Fund a restricted account known as the "In God We
             49      Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account."
             50          (2) The account shall be funded by:
             51          (a) contributions deposited into the account in accordance with Section 41-1a-422 ;
             52          (b) private contributions; and
             53          (c) donations or grants from public or private entities.
             54          (3) Upon appropriation by the Legislature, the department shall distribute funds in the
             55      account to one or more charitable organizations that:
             56          (a) qualify as being tax exempt under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code;
             57          (b) are located within the state and are not affiliated with a parent organization;
             58          (c) create or support a minimum of 20 educational programs and community events

             59      within the state that celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country;
             60          (d) celebrate, teach, honor, and strengthen the traditional American values of family,
             61      freedom, God, and country; and
             62          (e) focus primarily on sponsoring community events surrounding the July 4
             63      Independence Day holiday.
             64          (4) (a) An organization described in Subsection (3) may apply to the department to
             65      receive a distribution in accordance with Subsection (3).
             66          (b) An organization that receives a distribution from the department in accordance with
             67      Subsection (3) shall expend the distribution only to:
             68          (i) create or support educational programs and community events within the state that
             69      celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country;
             70          (ii) advertise to promote the educational and community events described in
             71      Subsection (4)(b)(i);
             72          (iii) sponsor youth speech, multimedia, and essay contests that encourage youth to
             73      honor families, freedom, God, and country; and
             74          (iv) pay the costs of issuing or reordering In God We Trust freedom celebration
             75      support special group license plate decals.
             76          (c) In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the
             77      department may make rules providing procedures for an organization to apply to the
             78      department to receive a distribution under this Subsection (3).
             79          (5) In accordance with Section 63J-1-602.1 , appropriations from the account are
             80      nonlapsing.
