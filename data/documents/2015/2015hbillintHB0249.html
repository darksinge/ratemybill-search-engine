8     LONG TITLE
9     General Description:
10          This bill amends provisions related to assisted reproduction.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     requires the donor assisting in reproduction to disclose their identifying information
14     and medical history to the fertility clinic;
15          ▸     provides an option for an affidavit of nondisclosure for the donor assisting in
16     reproduction; and
17          ▸     allows a person conceived through assisted reproduction access to the
18     nonidentifying medical history of the donor from the fertility clinic.
19     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
20          None
21     Other Special Clauses:
22          None
23     Utah Code Sections Affected:
24     ENACTS:
25          78B-15-708, Utah Code Annotated 1953
26     

27     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
28          Section 1. Section 78B-15-708 is enacted to read:
29          78B-15-708. Access to identifying information and medical history.
30          (1) A person who donates to a fertility clinic for the purpose of assisted reproduction
31     shall provide identifying information and a medical history to the fertility clinic. The fertility
32     clinic shall keep the identifying information and medical history of the donor and may disclose
33     the information as provided under Subsection (2).
34          (2) (a) A person conceived through assisted reproduction who is at least 18 years of age
35     shall be provided, upon the person's request, access to identifying information of the donor who
36     assisted in the reproduction process that resulted in the birth of the person, unless the donor
37     signed an affidavit of nondisclosure with the fertility clinic.
38          (b) Regardless of whether the donor signed an affidavit of nondisclosure, a person
39     conceived through assisted reproduction who is at least 18 years of age shall be provided, upon
40     the person's request, access to the nonidentifying medical history of the donor who assisted in
41     the reproduction process that resulted in the person's birth.
