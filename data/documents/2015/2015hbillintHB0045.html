7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill enacts language related to a municipal and county governing body.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     prohibits a municipal or county governing body from expelling a member of the
13     body from a public meeting or prohibiting the member from attending; and
14          ▸     makes technical corrections.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          None
19     Utah Code Sections Affected:
20     AMENDS:
21          10-3-607, as enacted by Laws of Utah 1977, Chapter 48
22     ENACTS:
23          17-53-206.5, Utah Code Annotated 1953
24     

25     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
26          Section 1. Section 10-3-607 is amended to read:
27          10-3-607. Expulsion of members prohibited -- Exception for disorderly conduct.
28          (1) Except as provided in Subsection (2), the governing body may not expel a member
29     of the governing body from a public meeting or prohibit the member from attending a public
30     meeting.
31          (2) The governing body of each municipality may fine or expel from a public meeting
32     any member of the governing body for disorderly conduct on a two-thirds vote of the members
33     of the governing body.
34          Section 2. Section 17-53-206.5 is enacted to read:
35          17-53-206.5. Expulsion of members prohibited.
36          The legislative body may not expel a member of the legislative body from a public
37     meeting or prohibit the member from attending a public meeting.
