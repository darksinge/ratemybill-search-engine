7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill creates a program to provide grants to local education agencies for
10     professional development for school counselors.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     defines terms;
14          ▸     creates the Strengthening College and Career Readiness Program, a grant program
15     for local education agencies, to improve students' college and career readiness
16     through enhancing the skill level of school counselors to provide college and career
17     counseling;
18          ▸     directs the State Board of Education to:
19               •     develop a certificate for school counselors that certifies that a school counselor
20     is highly skilled at providing college and career counseling;
21               •     award grants to local education agencies, on a competitive basis, for payment of
22     course fees for courses required to earn the certificate;
23               •     make rules; and
24               •     report to the Education Interim Committee.
25     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
26          This bill appropriates in fiscal year 2016:
27          ▸     to the State Board of Education - State Office of Education, as a one-time
28     appropriation:
29               •     from the Education Fund, One-time, $440,000
30     Other Special Clauses:
31          This bill provides a special effective date.

36     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
37          Section 1. Section 53A-15-1501 is enacted to read:
38          53A-15-1501. Initiative to strengthen college and career readiness.
39          (1) As used in this section:
40          (a) "College and career counseling" means:
41          (i) nurturing college and career aspirations;
42          (ii) assisting students in planning an academic program that connects to college and
43     career goals;
44          (iii) providing early and ongoing exposure to information necessary to make informed
45     decisions when selecting a college and career;
46          (iv) promoting participation in college and career assessments;
47          (v) providing financial aid information; and
48          (vi) increasing understanding about college admission processes.
49          (b) "LEA" or "local education agency" means a school district or charter school.
50          (2) There is created the Strengthening College and Career Readiness Program, a grant
51     program for LEAs, to improve students' college and career readiness through enhancing the
52     skill level of school counselors to provide college and career counseling.
53          (3) The State Board of Education shall:
54          (a) on or before August 1, 2015, collaborate with the Board of Regents, and business,
55     community, and education stakeholders to develop a certificate for school counselors that:
56          (i) certifies that a school counselor is highly skilled at providing college and career
57     counseling; and
58          (ii) is aligned with the Utah Comprehensive Counseling and Guidance Program as
59     defined in rules established by the State Board of Education;
60          (b) subject to legislative appropriations, award grants to LEAs, on a competitive basis,
61     for payment of course fees for courses required to earn the certificate developed by the State
62     Board of Education under Subsection (3)(a); and
63          (c) in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Administrative Rulemaking Act, make
64     rules specifying:
65          (i) procedures for applying for and awarding grants under this section;
66          (ii) criteria for awarding grants; and
67          (iii) reporting requirements for grantees.
68          (4) An LEA that receives a grant under this section shall use the grant for payment of
69     course fees for courses required to attain the certificate as determined by the State Board of
70     Education under Subsection (3)(a).
71          (5) The State Board of Education shall report to the Education Interim Committee on
72     the status of the Strengthening College and Career Readiness Program on or before:
73          (a) November 1, 2016; and
74          (b) November 1, 2017.
