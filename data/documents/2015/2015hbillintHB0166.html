7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill removes the name of a county clerk from the caption on a ballot and prohibits
10     a polling place from bearing the name of a candidate or a candidate's immediate family
11     member.
12     Highlighted Provisions:
13          This bill:
14          ▸     removes the name of a county clerk from the caption on a ballot;
15          ▸     prohibits a polling place from bearing the name of a candidate or a candidate's
16     immediate family member; and
17          ▸     makes technical corrections.
18     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
19          None
20     Other Special Clauses:
21          None
