7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill describes the procedure for selling, exchanging, or transferring state land.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     requires the governor, an executive agency, or a political subdivision to submit a
13     proposal to sell, exchange, or transfer 500 acres or more of state land for legislative
14     approval;
15          ▸     requires the governor, an executive agency, or a political subdivision to notify the
16     Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee of any
17     proposal to sell, exchange, or transfer less than 500 acres of state land; and
18          ▸     requires a political subdivision that enters into a discussion with a federal agent or
19     entity regarding land management issues to provide written notice to the
20     Legislature.
21     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
22          None
23     Other Special Clauses:
24          None
25     Utah Code Sections Affected:
26     ENACTS:
27          63L-6-106, Utah Code Annotated 1953

29     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
30          Section 1. Section 63L-6-106 is enacted to read:
31          63L-6-106. Legislative approval of land sales, exchanges, or transfers with the
32     federal government.
33          (1) The governor, an executive agency, or a political subdivision may not execute an
34     agreement to sell, exchange, or transfer 500 or more acres of state land, regardless of whether
35     the 500 acres are contiguous or noncontiguous, until:
36          (a) the governor, executive agency, or political subdivision submits the agreement or
37     proposed agreement to the Legislature for its approval or rejection; and
38          (b) the Legislature passes a joint resolution approving an agreement or proposed
39     agreement.
40          (2) The governor, an executive agency, or a political subdivision shall notify the
41     Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee before executing an
42     agreement to sell, exchange, or transfer less than 500 acres of state land.
43          (3) A political subdivision shall give written notice to the Legislative Management
44     Committee as soon as the political subdivision enters into a discussion with a federal agent or
45     entity regarding land management issues within the state.
