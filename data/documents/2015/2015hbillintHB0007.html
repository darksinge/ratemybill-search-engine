8     LONG TITLE
9     Committee Note:
10          The Executive Appropriations Committee recommended this bill.
11     General Description:
12          This bill appropriates funds for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal
13     year beginning July 1, 2015 and ending June 30, 2016.
14     Highlighted Provisions:
15          This bill:
16          ▸     provides appropriations for the use and support of certain state agencies; and,
17          ▸     provides appropriations for other purposes as described.
18     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
19          This bill appropriates $83,754,200 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2016,
20     including:
21          ▸     $36,323,100 from the General Fund;
22          ▸     $47,431,100 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
23          This bill appropriates $20,472,500 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2016.
24     Other Special Clauses:
25          This bill takes effect on July 1, 2015.
