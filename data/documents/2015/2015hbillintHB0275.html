7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Designation of State Highways Act by designating the Vietnam
10     Veterans Memorial Highway.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     designates the existing portions of Interstate 84 located within the state as the
14     Vietnam Veterans Memorial Highway; and
15          ▸     requires the Department of Transportation to make the designation of this highway
16     on future state highway maps.
17     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
18          None
19     Other Special Clauses:
20          None
21     Utah Code Sections Affected:
22     ENACTS:
23          72-4-215, Utah Code Annotated 1953
24     

25     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
26          Section 1. Section 72-4-215 is enacted to read:
27          72-4-215. Vietnam Veterans Memorial Highway.
28          (1) There is established the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Highway composed of the
29     existing portions of Interstate 84 located within the state.
30          (2) In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall
31     designate the portions of the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the Vietnam Veterans
32     Memorial Highway on future state highway maps.
