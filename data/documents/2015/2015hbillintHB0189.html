7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill amends provisions relating to the Dispute Resolution Account.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     increases the amount of fees deposited into the Dispute Resolution Account.
13     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
14          None
15     Other Special Clauses:
16          None
17     Utah Code Sections Affected:
18     AMENDS:
19          78B-6-209, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 22
20     

21     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
22          Section 1. Section 78B-6-209 is amended to read:
23          78B-6-209. Dispute Resolution Account -- Appropriation.
24          There is created a restricted account within the General Fund known as the "Dispute
25     Resolution Account." [Three] Five dollars of the fees established in Subsections
26     78A-2-301(1)(a) through (e), (1)(g), and (1)(s) shall be allocated to and deposited into the
27     [restricted account] Dispute Resolution Account. The Legislature shall annually appropriate
28     money from the Dispute Resolution Account to the Administrative Office of the Courts to
29     implement the purposes of Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 2, Alternative Dispute Resolution Act.
