7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill establishes that a voter's absentee status does not expire.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     establishes that a voter's absentee status does not expire; and
13          ▸     makes technical changes.
14     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
15          None
16     Other Special Clauses:
17          None
18     Utah Code Sections Affected:
19     AMENDS:
20          20A-3-304, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2013, Chapters 198, 218 and last
21     amended by Coordination Clause, Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 198
22     

23     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
24          Section 1. Section 20A-3-304 is amended to read:
25          20A-3-304. Application for absentee ballot -- Time for filing and voting.
26          (1) (a) Any registered voter who wishes to vote an absentee ballot may either:
27          (i) file an absentee ballot application:
28          (A) on the electronic system maintained by the lieutenant governor under Section
29     20A-2-206; or
30          (B) with the appropriate election officer for an official absentee ballot as provided in
31     this section; or
32          (ii) vote in person at the office of the appropriate election officer as provided in Section
33     20A-3-306.
34          (b) A person that collects a completed absentee ballot application from a registered
35     voter shall file the completed absentee ballot application with the appropriate election official
36     before the earlier of:
37          (i) 14 days after the day on which the registered voter signed the absentee ballot form;
38     or
39          (ii) the Thursday before the next election.
40          (2) As it relates to an absentee ballot application to be filled out entirely by the voter:
41          (a) except as provided in Subsection (2)(b), the lieutenant governor or election officer
42     shall approve an application form for absentee ballot applications in substantially the following
43     form:
44          "I, ____, a qualified elector, residing at ____ Street, ____ City, ____ County, Utah
45     apply for an official absentee ballot to be voted by me at the election.
46          Date ________ (month\day\year) Signed ___________________________
47                                             Voter"; and
48          (b) the lieutenant governor or election officer shall approve an application form for
49     regular primary elections and for the Western States Presidential Primary in substantially the
50     following form:
51          "I, ____, a qualified elector, residing at ____ Street, ____ City, ____ County, Utah
52     apply for an official absentee ballot for the _______________ political party to be voted by me
53     at the primary election.
54          I understand that I must be affiliated with or authorized to vote the political party's
55     ballot that I request.
56          Dated _________ (month\day\year) ____ Signed ___________________________
57                                                  Voter"
58          (c) If requested by the applicant, the election officer shall:
59          (i) mail or fax the application form to the absentee voter; or
60          (ii) deliver the application form to any voter who personally applies for it at the office
61     of the election officer.
62          (3) As it relates to an absentee ballot application to be filled out for, and finished and
63     signed by, a voter:
64          (a) except as provided in Subsection (3)(b), the lieutenant governor or election officer
65     shall approve an application form for absentee ballot applications in substantially the following
66     form:
67          "I, ____, a qualified elector, residing at ____ Street, ____ City, ____ County, Utah
68     apply for an official absentee ballot to be voted by me at the election.
69          I understand that a person that collects this absentee ballot application is required to file
70     it with the appropriate election official before the earlier of fourteen days after the day on
71     which I sign the application or the Thursday before the next election.
72          This form is provided by (insert name of person or organization).
73          I have verified that the information on this application is correct.
74          I understand that I will receive a ballot at the following address: (insert address and an
75     adjacent check box);
76          OR
77          I request that the ballot be mailed to the following address: (insert blank space for an
78     address and an adjacent check box).
79          Date ________ (month\day\year) Signed ___________________________
80          Voter"; and
81          (b) the lieutenant governor or election officer shall approve an application form for
82     regular primary elections and for the Western States Presidential Primary in substantially the
83     following form:
84          "I, ____, a qualified elector, residing at ____ Street, ____ City, ____ County, Utah
85     apply for an official absentee ballot for the _______________ political party to be voted by me
86     at the primary election.
87          I understand that I must be affiliated with or authorized to vote the political party's
88     ballot that I request. I understand that a person that collects this absentee ballot application is
89     required to file it with the appropriate election official before the earlier of fourteen days after
90     the day on which I sign the application or the Thursday before the next primary election.
91          This form is provided by (insert name of person or organization).
92          I have verified that the information on this application is correct.
93          I understand that I will receive a ballot at the following address: (insert address and an
94     adjacent check box);
95          OR
96          I request that the ballot be mailed to the following address: (insert blank space for an
97     address and an adjacent check box).
98          Dated _________ (month\day\year) ____ Signed ___________________________
99          Voter"
100          (4) The forms described in Subsections (2) and (3) shall contain instructions on how a
101     voter may cancel an absentee ballot application.
102          (5) Except as provided in Subsection 20A-3-306(2)(a), a voter who wishes to vote by
103     absentee ballot shall file the application for an absentee ballot with the lieutenant governor or
104     appropriate election officer no later than the Thursday before election day.
105          (6) (a) A county clerk [may] shall establish [a permanent] an absentee voter list[. (b)
106     The clerk shall place on the list] containing the name of [any person] each voter who:
107          (i) requests [permanent] absentee voter status; and
108          (ii) meets the requirements of this section.
109          (b) A county clerk may not remove a voter's name from the list described in Subsection
110     (6)(a) unless:
111          (i) the voter is no longer listed in the official register; or
112          (ii) the voter cancels the voter's absentee status.
113          (c) (i) Each year, the clerk shall mail a questionnaire to each [person] voter whose
114     name is on the absentee voter list.
115          (ii) The questionnaire shall allow the [absentee person to] voter to:
116          (A) verify the voter's residence[.]; or
117          (B) cancel the voter's absentee status.
118          [(iii) The clerk may remove the names of any voter from the absentee voter registration
119     list if:]
120          [(A) the voter is no longer listed in the official register; or]
121          [(B) the voter fails to verify the voter's residence and absentee status.]
122          (d) The clerk shall provide a copy of the [permanent] absentee voter list to election
123     officers for use in elections.
