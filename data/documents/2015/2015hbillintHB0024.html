8     Committee Note:

10     General Description:
11          This bill modifies the Insurance Code.
12     Highlighted Provisions:

14          ▸     amends definition provisions;

17               •     addressing subsidiaries;

19     domestic insurer;

23     system;
24               •     addressing examination of registered insurers;
25               •     providing for supervisory colleges;
26               •     addressing confidentiality of information;
27               •     imposing sanctions;

29               •     providing for recovery;

31               •     granting rulemaking authority and authority to issue orders;
32               •     addressing judicial review and mandamus;
33               •     addressing conflicts with other laws; and
34               •     providing for severability;

36               •     providing the scope of the chapter;

48          ▸     addresses license lapse and voluntary surrender;
49          ▸     amends unfair marketing practices to include the use of certain names;

52     information related to place of business and contact information;

55          ▸     modifies definition provisions related to captive insurance companies;

64          ▸     repeals language related to capital stock of a captive insurance company;
65          ▸     addresses when a captive insurance company can provide reinsurance;
66          ▸     addresses conversion or merger of a captive insurance company;
