7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Traffic Code by repealing restrictions on operating a motor
10     vehicle.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     repeals restrictions on an operator of a motor vehicle traveling through a defile or
14     canyon or on a mountain highway; and
15          ▸     repeals coasting restrictions on an operator of a motor vehicle.
16     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
17          None
18     Other Special Clauses:
19          None
20     Utah Code Sections Affected:
21     REPEALS:
22          41-6a-1708, as renumbered and amended by Laws of Utah 2005, Chapter 2
23          41-6a-1709, as renumbered and amended by Laws of Utah 2005, Chapter 2
24     

25     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
26          Section 1. Repealer.
27          This bill repeals:
28          Section 41-6a-1708, Driving in canyons and on mountain highways.
29          Section 41-6a-1709, Coasting prohibited.
