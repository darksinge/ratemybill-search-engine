7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Sex and Kidnap Offender Registry regarding reporting
10     requirements.
11     Highlighted Provisions:
12          This bill:
13          ▸     provides detail regarding reporting for a sex or kidnap offense committed outside of
14     Utah;
15          ▸     requires that the offender provide the phone number for any places where the
16     offender is employed or volunteering; and
17          ▸     includes citation cross references.
18     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
19          None
20     Other Special Clauses:
21          None

203     specify whether the youth offender is being committed for secure confinement or placement in
