7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill provides that Utah National Guard funds are nonlapsing.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     adds Utah National Guard funds to a list of nonlapsing funds.
13     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
14          None
15     Other Special Clauses:
16          None
