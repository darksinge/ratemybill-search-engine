7     LONG TITLE
8     Committee Note:
9          The Judiciary Interim Committee recommended this bill.
10     General Description:
11          This bill amends provisions related to the Jury and Witness Act to address jury service
12     requirements for specific counties.
13     Highlighted Provisions:
14          This bill:
15          ▸     exempts counties of the fourth, fifth, and sixth class and counties of the third class
16     with populations up to 75,000 from certain jury service requirements.
17     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
18          None
19     Other Special Clauses:
20          None
21     Utah Code Sections Affected:
22     AMENDS:
23          78B-1-110, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 202
24     

25     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
26          Section 1. Section 78B-1-110 is amended to read:
27          78B-1-110. Limitations on jury service.
28          (1) In any two-year period, a person may not:
29          [(1)] (a) be required to serve on more than one grand jury;
30          [(2)] (b) be required to serve as both a grand and trial juror;
31          [(3)] (c) be required to attend court for prospective jury service as a trial juror more
32     than one court day, except if necessary to complete service in a particular case; or
33          [(4)] (d) if summoned for prospective jury service and the summons is complied with
34     as directed, be selected for the qualified jury list more than once.
35          (2) (a) Subsection (1)(d) does not apply to counties of the fourth, fifth, and sixth class
36     and counties of the third class with populations up to 75,000.
37          (b) (i) All population figures used for this section shall be derived from the most recent
38     official census or census estimate of the United States Census Bureau.
39          (ii) If population estimates are not available from the United States Census Bureau,
40     population figures shall be derived from the estimate of the Utah Population Estimates
41     Committee.
