7     LONG TITLE
8     Committee Note:
9          The Business and Labor Interim Committee recommended this bill.
10     General Description:
11          This bill modifies oversight provisions to remove the insurance comparison tables
12     requirements from the sunset act.
13     Highlighted Provisions:
14          This bill:
15          ▸     removes the requirement that the Insurance Department produce certain comparison
16     tables from the Legislative Oversight and Sunset Act; and
17          ▸     makes technical changes.

19          None
20     Other Special Clauses:
21          None
