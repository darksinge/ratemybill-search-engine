7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies commercial hunting area provisions.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     modifies age requirements;
13          ▸     modifies hunter education requirements; and
14          ▸     makes technical changes.
15     Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16          None
17     Other Special Clauses:
18          None
19     Utah Code Sections Affected:
20     AMENDS:
21          23-17-6, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 297
22     

23     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
24          Section 1. Section 23-17-6 is amended to read:
25          23-17-6. Commercial hunting area -- Registration -- Requirements for hunters.
26          (1) (a) [Any] A person desiring to operate a commercial hunting area within [this] the
27     state to permit the releasing and shooting of pen-raised birds may apply to the Wildlife Board
28     for authorization to do so.
29          (b) The Wildlife Board may issue the applicant a certificate of registration to operate a
30     commercial hunting area in accordance with rules prescribed by the board in accordance with
31     Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act.
32          (c) The Wildlife Board may determine the number of commercial hunting areas that
33     may be established in each county of the state.
34          (2) [Any] (a) A certificate of registration issued under Subsection (1) shall specify the
35     species of birds that the applicant may propagate, keep, and release for shooting on the area
36     covered by the certificate of registration.
37          (b) The applicant may charge a fee for harvesting [these] the birds specified under
38     Subsection (2)(a).
39          (3) (a) [Any] A person hunting within the state on [any] a commercial hunting area
40     shall:
41          [(i) be at least 12 years old;]
42          [(ii)] (i) (A) possess proof of passing a division-approved hunter education course, if
43     the person was born after December 31, 1965; [and] or
44           (B) possess a trial hunting authorization issued under Section 23-19-14.6; and
45          [(iii)] (ii) have the permission of the owner or operator of the commercial hunting area.
46          (b) The operator of a commercial hunting area shall verify that each hunter on the
47     commercial hunting area meets the requirements of [Subsections] Subsection (3)(a)(i) [and
48     (3)(a)(ii)].
49          (4) Hunting on commercial hunting areas [shall be] is permitted only during the
50     commercial hunting area season prescribed by the Wildlife Board.
