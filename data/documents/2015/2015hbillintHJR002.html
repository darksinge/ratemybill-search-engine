21     Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
22          WHEREAS, pursuant to Utah Code Ann. Section 36-12-7, (1953), the Legislative
23     Management Committee has recommended the reappointment of Mr. John L. Fellows as
24     Legislative General Counsel for the Utah Legislature; and
25          WHEREAS, the reappointment of Mr. John L. Fellows in this position for a term of
26     office of six years beginning May 12, 2015, is subject to further approval of a majority vote of
27     both the House of Representatives and the Senate:
28          NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Legislature of the state of Utah that
29     the reappointment of Mr. John L. Fellows as Legislative General Counsel for the Utah
30     Legislature be approved for a six-year term of office beginning May 12, 2015.
