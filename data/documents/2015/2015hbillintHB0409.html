7     LONG TITLE
8     General Description:
9          This bill modifies the Utah Procurement Code.
10     Highlighted Provisions:
11          This bill:
12          ▸     modifies provisions relating to the procurement of the services of an architect or
13     engineer;
14          ▸     authorizes the head of a procurement unit with independent procurement authority
15     to address a procurement or contract that is out of compliance;
16          ▸     modifies a provision relating to exemptions from the procurement code;
17          ▸     modifies a provision relating to thresholds for small purchases;
18          ▸     modifies a provision relating to a multiple stage bidding process;
19          ▸     enacts a provision relating to changes in contract price;
20          ▸     modifies procurement appeal provisions relating to local government procurement
21     units; and
22          ▸     modifies provisions relating to the forfeiture of a security deposit or bond.
